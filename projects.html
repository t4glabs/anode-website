Strengthening Panchayats for Advocacy and Collaborative Engagement (SPACE)

Gumla, Hazaribagh and Koderma Districts, Jharkhand & Betul and Dindori Districts, Madhya Pradesh
Concluded (2015- 2019)
Implementation partner: PRADAN (Professional Assistance for Development Action)
We worked closely with PRADAN to deploy and develop the various stages of the GPOD framework across 15 GPs over three years to strengthen Gram Panchayats and improve their collaboration with Community Based Organisations. We saw impact across these project geographies with GPs leveraging funds and collaborating with government departments for irrigation infrastructure, accessing various inputs for landless and low income farmers. We also witnessed GP’s spearheading initiatives to improve dropout rates in schools.

Improving local governance institutions & democratic processes in Karnataka

Chitradurga and Challakere Blocks, Chitradurga District, Karnataka
Ongoing (2022- 2025)
Financial Partner: Azim Premji Foundation
Anode is building the capacity of elected members across 15 GPs and supporting them in implementation over a 3-year structured journey (GPOD Framework). This will enable Gram Panchayats to be effective units of self-governance and optimise their service delivery for citizens. In addition, we are facilitating effective collaboration between Gram Panchayats and citizens, through our work with the local community in the project areas.

Pilot of digitised Organisation Mapping Tool in two Panchayats of

Kolar and Tumkur Districts, Karnataka
Concluded (2022)
Implementation Partners: TIDE, Gram Vikas and MoU with Department of Panchayat Raj, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (RDPR), Government of Karnataka
To explore the possibility of integrating Anode’s GP Organisation Mapping Tool with Government of Karnataka’s revised online platform (Panchatantra 2.0), we collaborated for pilots in 2 GPs of. This pilot helped us develop accessible content and an IT platform for Gram Panchayats to self-assess the strengths and required improvements for their institutions.

Programme Strategy Paper for Sightsavers to engage with PRIs For 8 States

Concluded/ Completed (2022)
Client Partner: Sightsavers
Sightsavers has been working in India since 1966 to eliminate avoidable blindness and to ensure support to people who are irreversibly blind. They focus on collaborating with state governments to scale up operations for eye health, inclusive education and social inclusion. In order to strategise about collaborations with Panchayati Raj Institutions to scale up Sightsavers operations, Anode drafted a Programme Implementation Strategy Paper.

This included analysis of PRI mandates related to Sightsavers focus areas across their 8 intervention states, toolkits and processes for field teams to capture relevant data as well as recommendations for bringing PRIs as principles of programme implementation.

Sachet Pariyojana

Barwani, District, Madhya Pradesh
Ongoing (2021- 2023)
Implementation partner: Transform Rural India Foundation (TRIF)
To strengthen local democracy processes in Rajpur Block (Mission Antyodaya Block) in MP, TRIF has collaborated with multiple partners including Anode. This project aims to empower communities to ensure their demands are incorporated in their village development plans, strengthen Gram Panchayats to deliver the community’s demands and also improve collaboration of GPs with block and district governments.

Gram Spandan

Deoli, Seloo and Wardha Blocks, Wardha District, Maharashtra
Ongoing (2021- 2024)
Implementation partners: Kamalnayan Jamnalal Bajaj Foundation(KJBF) & Mission Samriddhi (MS)
KJBF has been working in the areas of sustainable agriculture, natural resource management and livelihoods in Wardha district. In order to mainstream this work, strengthen GPs and potentially leverage resources from different schemes KJBF, MS and Anode have partnered to implement the GPOD framework in 15 GPs. At this stage, we are conducting vision and mission workshops with GP members and citizens to identify their broad goals.
